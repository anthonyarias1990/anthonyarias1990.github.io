Welcome to Cutwater Rentals and Property Management

Homes for Rent

Above Expectations

Since 1990, Cutwater has owned, managed, constructed, and rented properites on the Island of Galveston. We personally vet all our homes listed in our rental portfolio to ensure you have an experience of the highest quality year after year

Rental Coordination

Every year, we handle inquiries, negotiations, and closings of rentals for over 8 houses in the Galveston. Over the years, we have processes 1000s of these types of communications and can ensure that your rental management experience is smooth and successful from an inquiry call to contract signature.

Consulting for Rules and Regulations

Owning a second home is complicated enough, and renting this home out can add an extra layer of complications. You can count on Cutwater to consult with you and ensure you understand any and all regulations and rules issued by Galveston County that affect your vacation home. This also includes rules and regulations related to construction, purchasing of homes, etc.

Rentals Maintenance and Cleaning

Renting a home can cause a lot of damage quickly to your home if you're not attentive to repairs, maintaining cleanliness, etc. Cutwater has a hardworking, detail-oriented team that is on call to clean and maintain homes before, during, and after vacation rentals.
